Electronic Device Loan Agreement
Please read the full policy before continuing. Summer 2026

The Cuesta College Library has a limited number of Chromebooks, Hotspots, Specialty Laptops, and Calculators available for checkout by enrolled Cuesta College students.

Students may check out one Hotspot plus one Calculator plus one Chromebook OR one Laptop at a time. Each device comes with a carrying case and charging/power cable (except calculators).

Academic Use Only

All electronic devices are intended for ACADEMIC USE ONLY. Use of these devices must follow the Student Code of Conduct and related technology/acceptable use policies. Activity on college devices and networks may be monitored.

Checkout Period

All devices are loaned for one semester or session. Devices must be returned before or by the last day of the semester/session/rotation. Devices are not checked out over semester breaks.

No Renewals

Renewals are not allowed. Extensions may be given on a case-by-case basis. Email extension requests to circulation@cuesta.edu.

Return Policy

Devices must be returned in-person during open hours. Devices will be inspected for damage upon return. Devices must be in clean, working order or a fee may be assessed. Do NOT return devices in book drops.

Late Fees & Lost Devices

There is no grace period for late fees. If a device is more than two weeks late, it will be considered lost and the student will be responsible for the full replacement cost.

Damage & Theft

Students are responsible for any costs associated with damage, loss, or theft of devices and accessories. Stolen or lost items must be reported to the local police department within 48 hours and to Library Staff at circulation@cuesta.edu.

Replacement Costs

  • Chromebook: $310 total (device $250, case $30, charger $30)
  • Specialty Laptop: $1,537 total (device $1,450, case $30, adapter $57)
  • WiFi Hotspot: $90 total (device $70, case $5, cable $15)
  • TI-30XIIS Calculator: $25
  • TI-30XS Calculator: $35
  • TI-83 Plus Calculator: $135

Waitlist

If the library is out of a device, you will be placed on a waitlist. You will be notified once when a device becomes available. You will have one week to pick up the device. If you do not pick it up within one week, your spot will be released to the next person and you must rejoin the waitlist.

Email circulation@cuesta.edu for availability or questions.
